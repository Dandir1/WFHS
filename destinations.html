Destinations

Destination Experience Facts
Moon
 Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Moon. Walk on its surface and witness Earthrise from a unique vantage point.
  1. Distance from Earth: 238,900 mi
  2. Moon travels around the Earth in an oval-shaped orbit
  3. It takes 27.3 days for the Moon to travel all the way around the Earth
  4. The Moon controls the tides
Mars
 Embark on a journey to the red planet. Explore its rugged terrain and learn about potential colonization efforts.
  1. Distance from Earth: 155.75 million mi
  2. Mars is sometimes called the Red Planet
  3. Mars has 4 seasons like Earth
  4. Mars has 2 moons called Deimos and Phobos and is named after the Roman god of war
Europa - Jupitar's Moon
 Discover the icy moon of Jupiter with its subsurface ocean. Experience the thrill of exploring uncharted territories.
  1. Distance from Earth: 390.4 million mi
  2. Europa's surface is made of ice, so it reflects 5.5 times the sunlight than Earth's Moon does
  3. Scientists believe that there lies an ocean beneath the surface of Europa, which houses life
  4. Because Europa's bombarded by the radiation from Jupiter, it glows in the dark.
