Our Story

Once upon a time, amidst the boundless expanse of the cosmos, a group of visionary souls united with a shared dream. Inspired by the mysteries of the universe and the enchanting beauty of Earth, they embarked on a daring quest to create a bridge between the heavens and humanity.

Guided by their unwavering passion, they delved deep into the realms of science, engineering, and exploration. As they unraveled the secrets of the five layers that enshroud our planet in the embrace of the atmosphere, they yearned to bring the sixth layer into existence – a realm where ordinary souls could traverse the celestial realm and witness the cosmic wonders firsthand.

With every challenge they faced, they persevered, driven by an insatiable curiosity and an unyielding belief in the power of human ingenuity. Today, their legacy lives on, as intrepid explorers from all walks of life embark on extraordinary journeys through the cosmos.

The story of our founding serves as a testament to the indomitable spirit of humanity and the profound connection we share with the universe.
